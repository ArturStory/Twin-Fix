How It Works

Create an account to get started. Each user is assigned a role (Reporter, Technician, Operator, or Admin) that determines their access and capabilities.

Document the issue with a detailed description, location information, and images. Use the interactive map to pinpoint the exact location.

Operators review and assign issues to the appropriate technicians based on priority and expertise.

Technicians address the issue, update its status, and document the repair. Reporters can track progress and provide feedback.

User Roles

Reporter

Submit issues, track your reports, and receive updates on resolution progress.

Technician

Receive assigned tasks, update status, add repair notes, and mark issues as resolved.

Operator

Review incoming reports, assign issues to technicians, and monitor overall maintenance status.

Administrator

Manage users, generate reports, oversee the system, and access all platform features.
